💡 USAGE
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

TO TOGGLE THEME:
   1. Look at the top navigation bar
   2. Find the moon icon (🌙) on the right side
   3. Click it
   4. Theme switches to dark mode
   5. Icon changes to sun (☀️)
   6. Click again to switch back

TO CHANGE LANGUAGE:
   1. Click the globe icon (🌐 EN ▼)
   2. Select your language
   3. Website updates

TO LOGIN:
   1. Click the shield icon (🛡️)
   2. Enter credentials
   3. Access admin panel
