💡 DESIGN PRINCIPLES APPLIED
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

1. CLEAR HIERARCHY:

   • Secondary actions (Theme, Language) further left

   • Clear visual separation

4. RESPONSIVE STRATEGY:
   • Desktop: Maximum spacing for clarity
   • Tablet: Balanced spacing with hamburger menu
   • Mobile: Compact but usable layout
