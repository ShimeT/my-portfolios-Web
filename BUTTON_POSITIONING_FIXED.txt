═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════
💡 DESIGN RATIONALE
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

WHY SMALLER ADMIN BUTTON?
• Less prominent (it's hidden by default anyway)
• Doesn't compete with Register button
• More subtle and professional
• Saves space for other elements

WHY POSITION TO THE LEFT?
• Register button is primary action for users
• Admin button is secondary (for you only)
• Natural reading order (left to right)
• Prevents accidental admin clicks

WHY MAINTAIN SPACING?
• Touch targets need 44px minimum
• Prevents fat-finger errors
• Professional appearance
• Clear visual separation
